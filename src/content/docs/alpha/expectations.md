MUSE is in **private alpha**. It's feature-rich but still early, so here's what to expect.

## This is an alpha

Expect rough edges, occasional bugs, and interface details that will change. **Do not use
MUSE as the sole home for irreplaceable work during alpha** — always keep copies of anything
you cannot afford to lose. See [Backups & safety](/manual/backups-and-safety/) for how MUSE
protects your work.

## Releases

- **Cadence:** expect a new build roughly every 2–3 weeks.
- **Project files:** the `.muse` file format is stable during alpha — your projects should
  keep opening across builds. Settings and interface state may reset between versions.
- **Updates:** there's no in-app auto-updater yet, so you'll download new builds manually.
  You'll be notified when a new build is available.
